Comprehensive Services Offered
Comprehensive Eye Check-ups 01

Thorough, state-of-the-art visual evaluations to ensure optimal eye health, prescribe accurate vision correction, and detect potential issues early.

Cataract Surgery (Phaco) 02

Advanced Phacoemulsification and premium Intraocular Lenses (IOLs) for quick, painless, and highly precise vision restoration.

Glaucoma Treatment 03

Advanced tonometry and optic nerve imaging to accurately diagnose, manage, and protect your sight from glaucoma-related damage.

Retina & Diabetic Eye Care 04

Specialized screening, injections, and laser treatments designed to protect against and treat diabetes-related vision loss and retinal disorders.

Pediatric Eye Care 05

Gentle, expert care tailored specifically for children, addressing developmental vision issues, refractive errors, and eye health.

Squint Evaluation 06

Comprehensive surgical and non-surgical therapies to correct eye alignment, restoring both appearance and binocular vision.

LASIK & Refractive Services 07

Modern laser vision correction procedures designed to help you live a clear, vibrant life entirely free from glasses and contacts.

Optical & Contact Lenses 08

A premium selection of designer frames, specialized lightweight lenses, and expert contact lens fitting directly inside the hospital.

Emergency Eye Care 09

Immediate trauma response and urgent care protocols for acute ocular conditions, infections, and accidental eye injuries.
